Jurdle...How To Play

How to Play Jurdle

  • Guess a five letter word by typing each letter into the boxes.

  • Each guess must be a valid word.

  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • If a letter is in the word and the correct spot, it will appear green.

  • If a letter is in the word but not in the correct spot, it will appear yellow.

  • If a letter is not in the word, it will appear grey.
